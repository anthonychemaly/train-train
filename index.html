About

Bureau Antoine Matta is an international architectural practice based in Beirut, Lebanon. The office was founded by Antoine Matta in 1963 as an interior design firm but in 2017 extended it's expertise to architecture and managing services. Antoine Matta took on many interior design projects, private residences in Greece, Paris, London and throughout the Middle-East.
